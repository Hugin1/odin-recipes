A simple recipe for fried rice

A very tasty meal that meal that would quill your hunger and make your day.

A very easy dish to prepare and does not require that much to prepare like our other recipes

The fried rice is a traditional chinese food that make you saltivate by only thinking about it, one bite is enough to make you devour the whole dish, above all it is easy to prepare.

    The Ingriedients needed to prepare the dish are the following :
  • rice
  • carrots
  • peas
  • corn
  • papprika
  • chicken
  • various speices in addition to salt and pepper
  • half a cup of oil
    Now for the steps in order to make the fried rice :
  1. First boil the rice for 10-15 minutes
  2. While the ricw boils cut all the other vegetables to small pieces
  3. Put a small pot on the stove and put the half cup of oil there on a low fire
  4. After the rice is ready bring a big fry pan with a little oil or butter on it and put the rice and various vegetabels in it
  5. Mix the rice and other Ingiedients together on a low heat and add the speices then to the mix
  6. Put the meal in an appropiriate dish and enjoy the meal
