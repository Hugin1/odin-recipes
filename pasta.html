Pasta recipe

An easy recipe for students and workers who do not have much time but want to save money by coocking home food, a pasta that will make your day

There is no one who does not love pasta since it has a deep history and roots in the human society, a bite equal a lesson in the italian history

The pasta can have also a good effect for those who do not know how to put healthy green in the food of the children. you can simply accomplish this by adding the vegetables to the sauce of the pasta, not 100% healthy but it is at least a step forwar.

    The Ingredients needed for the project pasta :
  • Pasta - *any shape you like*
  • pasta sauce
  • pepper and salt
  • chicken meat
  • vegetables
    Now to get near the gas and start coocking, here are the steps you neeed to follow :
  1. First put water in an approperiate vessel with enough water and boil it for 15 minutes
  2. Put the vegetabels and the chicken meatin another pot and cook it for 15-25 minutes, do not forget to add the fitting speices for the dish
  3. Seperate the hot water from the pasta then put it agian in the post with low heat and then add the sauce with the other Ingredients from the other pot
  4. Increase the heat and add a bit of salt and pepper, remember not to add too much, now stirr all together and mix them in harmoney
  5. Put the finished meal in a beautiful plate and enjoy your meal .
