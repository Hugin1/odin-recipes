pizza recipe

An easy recipe for beginners, pizza with salami and peperoni and corn which will make you drool

A very easy recipe for beginners to prepare , you only need a few things in order to prepare such a feast for the eyes and the stomach .

Not the most healthy meal for our cutomers who suffer from overweight, recommended for employers and students who do not have time to cook .

    Ingredients needed for the pizza :
  • pepperoni
  • tomato
  • wheat
  • salami - if preferred
  • corn
  • olive
  • tomato sauce
  • baking powder
  • eggs
    Steps in order to create the pizza
  1. First mix the water with the baking powder and eggs until you get dough
  2. Then wait until the dough get's bigger, preffered time of waiting is 15-30 minutes
  3. Make the dough thin and like a circle
  4. Put the ingredients that you wish to decorate the pizza with on the dough, that has taken tha shape of a circle
  5. put the pizza in the oven then wait for 15-30 minutes
  6. Now you have a pizza, Enjoy
